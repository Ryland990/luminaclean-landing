Best Duplicate Photo Cleaner Apps for iPhone in 2025

Your iPhone camera is incredible, but it comes with a downside: thousands of photos piling up, many of them duplicates or near-identical shots. While Apple's built-in Duplicates album (iOS 16+) handles exact copies, it misses similar photos, blurry images, and screenshot clutter. That is where a dedicated duplicate photo cleaner app comes in.

We tested the most popular photo cleaner apps for iPhone in 2025, evaluating them on duplicate detection accuracy, ease of use, privacy practices, and value for money. Here are our top picks.

2. Gemini Photos

2 Gemini Photos by MacPaw

Gemini Photos, made by the team behind CleanMyMac, is a well-established photo cleaner with a polished interface. It detects duplicates, similar photos, blurry shots, and screenshots, and organizes them into clear categories for review.

The app does a solid job of identifying similar photos and grouping them together. Its "Similar" category is particularly useful for people who take multiple shots of the same scene. However, the app requires a subscription for full functionality, which may not appeal to everyone. The free version is quite limited, essentially serving as a preview of the paid experience.

Key features:

  • Duplicate and similar photo detection
  • Blurry photo and screenshot identification
  • Clean, swipe-based interface
  • Notes and other media cleanup
Price: Subscription ($2.99/month or $19.99/year) Privacy: On-device processing iOS: 15.0+

Gemini Photos is a strong option if you are already in the MacPaw ecosystem or do not mind a subscription. The detection quality is good, though we found it occasionally less thorough than LuminaClean when it came to near-identical images with slight cropping or filter differences.

3. Remo Duplicate Photos Remover

3 Remo Duplicate Photos Remover

Remo focuses specifically on duplicate detection rather than trying to be an all-in-one photo organizer. It scans your library for exact and near-exact duplicates and presents them in a straightforward list for review.

The app is simple and does what it promises. Scanning speed is reasonable, and the results are generally accurate for true duplicates. Where Remo falls short compared to the top picks is in detecting similar (but not identical) photos and in its somewhat dated interface. It also does not offer blurry photo detection or screenshot cleanup.

Key features:

  • Exact and near-exact duplicate detection
  • Simple, focused interface
  • Batch deletion support
Price: Free with in-app purchases Privacy: On-device processing iOS: 14.0+

Remo is a decent choice if you specifically need to find exact duplicate photos and want a no-frills experience. For broader cleanup needs, however, the top two apps offer significantly more value.

4. Smart Cleaner

4 Smart Cleaner

Smart Cleaner takes a broader approach, combining photo cleanup with contact management, calendar cleanup, and a storage analyzer. If you want an all-in-one device maintenance app, Smart Cleaner covers a lot of ground.

On the photo side, it handles duplicates, similar photos, and screenshots. The detection is adequate, though we noticed it was less precise than LuminaClean or Gemini when dealing with photos that were similar but not identical. The contact merge feature is a nice bonus if your contacts list is messy.

The main drawback is the aggressive monetization. The app pushes its subscription hard, with frequent prompts and limited free functionality. Some users may also be uncomfortable with the broad permissions the app requests for its various cleanup features.

Key features:

  • Photo duplicate and similar detection
  • Contact merge and cleanup
  • Calendar event cleanup
  • Storage usage analyzer
Price: Subscription ($4.99/week or $24.99/year) Privacy: Varies by feature iOS: 15.0+

Smart Cleaner is best suited for users who want a single app that handles multiple types of device cleanup. However, if photo management is your primary concern, one of the dedicated photo cleaners above will deliver better results at a lower cost.
