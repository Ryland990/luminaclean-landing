Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

LuminaClean ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our iOS application handles information when you use our photo cleaning service.

Our Privacy Promise

LuminaClean is designed with privacy as a core principle. We want to be completely transparent: we do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data or photos.

How LuminaClean Works

LuminaClean uses Apple's on-device machine learning technology to analyze your photo library. This means:

  • All photo analysis happens entirely on your iPhone
  • Your photos never leave your device
  • No images are uploaded to any server
  • The app works fully offline (airplane mode compatible)

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Your photos or images
  • Photo metadata (location, date, camera info)
  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Any information about which photos you delete

Photo Library Access

LuminaClean requires access to your Photo Library to function. This permission is used solely to:

  • Scan your photos to detect duplicates, similar images, blurry photos, and screenshots
  • Display photos within the app for your review
  • Delete photos you choose to remove (with your explicit confirmation)

This access is processed entirely on your device and no photo data is ever transmitted externally.

No Account Required

LuminaClean does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use the app.

Third-Party Services

LuminaClean does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Security

Since we don't collect or store any data, there is no data to secure on our end. Your photos remain protected by your device's built-in security features and Apple's iOS security framework.

Children's Privacy

LuminaClean does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.
